# My Launch Video Concept

Product name: Health Insight AI

App URL:https://insight-nurse-bot.lovable.app  

My concept title: Calm Confidence Before Care

Target viewer: A student or young adult who feels unsure about their symptoms and wants help organizing what to do next.

Story angle: Health concerns can feel overwhelming, but Health Insight AI helps users turn confusing symptoms into clearer next steps.

Tone: Calm, reassuring, credible, and simple.

Opening hook: “Not sure if your symptoms are worth a visit?”

Main product moment to show: A user enters their symptoms into Health Insight AI and receives organized guidance with suggested next steps and reminders that it is not a diagnosis.

What the viewer should understand: Health Insight AI is an educational support tool that helps users prepare for care and make more informed decisions, but it does not replace a doctor.

Call to action: Check your symptoms with more confidence.

What I want to avoid: Overpromising, scary medical visuals, making it seem like the app gives an official diagnosis, or using a robotic tone.
