# Competitive Landscape

| Competitor | What They Do | Strength | Weakness |
|------------|-------------|----------|----------|
| ChatGPT | General-purpose AI assistant that can answer health-related questions in a conversational way | Fast, familiar, and flexible for open-ended questions | Not built specifically for health navigation, does not clearly communicate medical credibility, and can blur the line between general AI help and clinically grounded guidance |
| Ada | Symptom-checking health platform that helps users understand possible conditions and care pathways | Purpose-built for health use cases and already recognized as a symptom-checking option | Feels more like a structured checker than a personalized health companion, and may feel less conversational or less tailored to ongoing user context |
| Reddit | Community forum where users search symptoms, experiences, and advice from other people | Feels authentic and relatable because advice comes from real people | Information quality is inconsistent, can intensify panic, and often lacks medical credibility or personalized context |

## Our Differentiation
Health Insight AI aims to sit between generic AI chat and unreliable internet searching by combining conversational symptom support with a more credible, privacy-aware, and persistent health experience. The product is designed to help users feel prepared rather than panicked, while preserving history, profile context, and a clearer bridge to real care.
