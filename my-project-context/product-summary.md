# Product Summary

## Product Name
Health Insight AI

## What It Does
Health Insight AI is a digital health companion that uses a multimodal AI model to provide conversational health information. The app helps users understand possible causes for symptoms, identify health risks, and receive preventative care guidance in a more interactive format than a standard search engine.

## Who It Is For
The product is designed for people who are unsure whether their symptoms require a doctor's visit, feel intimidated by clinical environments, or do not currently have a primary care provider. It is also especially relevant for younger users who want to explore health concerns privately before speaking with parents or guardians, and for people trying to avoid unnecessary medical costs while still making informed decisions.

## Problem Being Solved
The app is meant to reduce panic and confusion caused by frantic online symptom searches that often surface worst-case scenarios and misinformation. Instead of forcing users to piece together static health articles on their own, AI Health Insights offers a guided conversation that gives context, prepares users for real appointments, and lowers the barrier to seeking appropriate care.

## Value Proposition
Health Insight AI helps users move from feeling stressed to feeling prepared. Rather than dumping generic diagnoses on the user, it offers a more specialized and contextual symptom discussion informed by credible health research and physician insight. The product also supports users with physician reviews, visit summaries they can bring to appointments, nearby doctor recommendations filtered by insurance and preferences, and privacy-focused controls such as anonymous use and chat deletion.

## Current Status
The app is live at https://insight-nurse-bot.lovable.app and supports a working end-to-end user flow from signup to symptom input to AI-generated insights. It uses persistent data storage so profiles and conversation history remain available across sessions and devices.

## The One-Liner
Health Insight AI is a health chatbot that helps people understand symptoms, assess possible next steps, and feel more prepared before seeking medical care.
