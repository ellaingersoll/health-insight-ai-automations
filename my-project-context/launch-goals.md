# Launch Goals

## Target Users
The first users are classmates, friends, family members, and other early testers who are willing to try a guided symptom-support chatbot and share feedback on the experience.

## Timeline
This week, as part of the BADM 350 pre-launch automation sprint.

## Success Metric
5 completed symptom chats from real users.

## Definition of "Launched"
At least 10 people try Health Insight AI through the live app, and at least 5 of those users complete a full symptom chat from input to AI-generated output.

## Open Questions
- How should the product explain its difference from ChatGPT more clearly on first use?
- Should the team move to a feature-gated guest experience to reduce signup friction?
- Which trust signals should be most visible to new users: citations, security, physician-informed positioning, or all three?
