# 5-Post Teaser Content Calendar

## Campaign Strategy
**Goal:** Drive trial and clarify product differentiation before broader sharing  
**Audience:** Students, family, friends, and early testers who want a calmer and more credible first step for symptom support  
**Platforms:** LinkedIn, Instagram, X  
**Timeline:** 5 posts across the current launch week

---

## Post 1: The Problem
**Theme:** Symptom searching often creates panic instead of clarity  
**LinkedIn:** Most people start with a search engine when symptoms show up, but that usually means worst-case scenarios, conflicting advice, and more anxiety. We are building Health Insight AI to create a calmer, more guided first step.  
**Instagram:** Symptom search should not feel like a panic spiral. We are building something better. #HealthTech #StudentBuild  
**X:** Searching symptoms online usually creates more panic, not more clarity. We are building a calmer first step. #HealthTech  
**Suggested visual:** Split-screen of chaotic search results versus a calm guided chat interface

## Post 2: The Tease
**Theme:** Introduce the product without over-explaining  
**LinkedIn:** What if symptom support felt conversational, private, and actually helpful before your appointment? Health Insight AI is designed to help users feel more prepared, not more overwhelmed.  
**Instagram:** A smarter first step when you are not sure what your symptoms mean. Early access is open. #HealthInsightAI  
**X:** What if symptom support felt more guided and less overwhelming? That is what we are building. #AIHealth  
**Suggested visual:** Product screenshot with a short promise statement

## Post 3: Social Proof
**Theme:** Share early feedback and what it taught the team  
**LinkedIn:** Early testers told us the core flow works well and the chat history feels useful, but they also pushed us on an important question: how is this different from ChatGPT? That feedback is shaping our messaging right now.  
**Instagram:** Real feedback from early testers: the experience feels intuitive, but we need to make the difference from generic AI even clearer. #BuildInPublic  
**X:** Early feedback: intuitive flow, strong persistence, clearer differentiation needed. Good signal, clear next move. #BuildInPublic  
**Suggested visual:** Quote card highlighting the ChatGPT comparison insight

## Post 4: Behind the Scenes
**Theme:** Show the credibility and security work  
**LinkedIn:** Building in health means product trust matters as much as product polish. We have been tightening session handling, access controls, ownership checks, and prompt-injection defenses as we refine the user experience.  
**Instagram:** Not glamorous, but important: security hardening, trust signals, and cleaner health UX. #HealthProduct #AITrust  
**X:** Real health tools need more than a nice UI. We are working on security, trust, and clarity too. #AITrust  
**Suggested visual:** Build-board graphic with trust, privacy, and credibility themes
