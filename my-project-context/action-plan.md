# User Feedback Action Plan

**Product:** Health Insight AI  
**Feedback Source:** Early user testing  
**Total Items Analyzed:** 8

---

## Priority 1: Make the value proposition unmistakable
**Impact:** Helps new users immediately understand why this is not just another generic AI chatbot and increases the odds that they try the product seriously.  
**Root Cause:** Product differentiation versus ChatGPT is not obvious enough on first interaction.  
**Specific Action:** Add a clear above-the-fold message explaining that the app is built for symptom support, persistence, medical credibility, and preparation for care. Reinforce this with trust cues like citations, security language, and clinician-summary positioning.  
**Effort:** Half-day  
**Evidence:** "I was not sure how it was different from ChatGPT or another AI platform."

## Priority 2: Design a feature-gated guest experience
**Impact:** Reduces signup friction and gives users a reason to care before creating an account.  
**Root Cause:** The logged-out experience currently blocks users before they can feel any product value.  
**Specific Action:** Prototype a guest flow that allows one limited symptom interaction or a preview experience, while reserving saved history, clinician summaries, and personalized continuity for authenticated users.  
**Effort:** Multi-day  
**Evidence:** Multiple testers discussed a feature-gated model as a way to compete more effectively with other AI platforms.

## Priority 3: Protect and highlight the strengths already working
**Impact:** Preserves the parts of the app users already find intuitive and useful.  
**Root Cause:** It would be easy to over-focus on differentiation and accidentally complicate the experience.  
**Specific Action:** Keep the current clean flow, preserve organized history and persistence, and make those strengths more visible in the interface and launch messaging.  
**Effort:** Quick fix (< 1 hour) for messaging, ongoing for product decisions  
**Evidence:** Testers completed the core flow successfully and responded positively to history organization and persistent profile context.

---

## Quick Wins (Can Do Today)
- Add a short comparison line that explains how Health Insight AI differs from generic AI tools
- Surface trust signals such as citations, privacy, and persistence earlier in the flow
- Turn the guest-versus-authenticated feature matrix into a visible product decision document
- Use early tester language in launch messaging to emphasize real strengths

## Board Memo Talking Point
"We analyzed 8 pieces of early feedback and found that the core flow is working, but product differentiation is our biggest near-term risk. Our top priority is clarifying why Health Insight AI is more credible, persistent, and health-specific than a general AI chatbot, while exploring a feature-gated guest model to reduce signup friction."
